Pizza

Pizza but made by you so it's better.

Ingredients:

  • 1,5 glass of flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 0,5 glass of water
  • 50 g of yeast or 25 i don't remember
  • tomato sauce
  • oregano
  • basil
  • mozzarella
  • other stuff you want

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 240 degrees of Celcius
  2. Add flour and a pinch of salt to the bowl and mix them.
  3. Mix yeast with water
  4. Add mixed yeast to the bawl with flour
  5. Mix it
  6. Spread the dough on something you'll put in the oven
  7. Spread tomato sauce on the dough
  8. Add other ingredients
  9. Put it all in the oven for 20 minutes
  10. Done
