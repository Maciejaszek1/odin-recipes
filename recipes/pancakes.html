Pancakes

Pancakes, a lot of pancakes.

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 glasses of flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 4-5 glasses of water
  • some icing sugar
  • some oil

Steps:

  1. Add flour, oil, water, eggs and icing sugar to a bowl and mix it.
  2. Pour oil on frying pan and pour some dough.
  3. Fry the dough until it's done.
  4. Done.
